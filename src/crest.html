Crest Consulting Website

A three-page website for Crest Consulting LLC, a business firm.

The Details:


This was a website that I created for a project in my WEB 2890 class at Weber State University. For this particular assignment, the target audience was for banking companies and firms that Crest Consulting has business with. They required a newer website that looked more simple yet still contained all the right info they had supplied in the description of the requirements.

The Method:


Those requirements listed were very specific, for example; they wanted the search bar accessible from every page of their website so that when/if a banking firm were on their website, they could easily find what they were looking for. They also wanted many things such as a very responsive website, all the info under certain sections, cooler colors (such as blue and turquoise), etc. This would require the use of Sass, CSS, HTML 5, and more tools to get the website very responsive.

The End Result:


After completing the website, I definitely learned a lot about how difficult it can be to properly set up content on the page. A good example of that would be making the navbar lower with many different sets of information. I honestly really struggle with animations but I'm getting used to it, it just takes a lot of practice and determination.
